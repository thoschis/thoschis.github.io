Description:
The game Galapagos is based on 9 different modular stones that can be combined with each other in numerous ways. It is made to write, build and invent. 
There are 45 stones in one set. Each stone has a size of 7,5 × 7,5 × 1,5 centimeter. The stones are made of white and red polyester.

Specs:
45 stones of 7,5 x 7,5 x 1,5 cm.
Polyester with white and red pigment.
